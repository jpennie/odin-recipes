Tacos

Tacos make people happy.. quick.. easy.. delicious..

Ingredients

  • Soft shells
  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • taco sauce
  • ground meat
  • avocados

Steps:

  1. cook meat until golden brown
  2. add meat spice and some water
  3. cook until no more liquid
  4. prepare extras
  5. heat the soft shells
  6. make them up

Enjoy! Every bite is so good.
